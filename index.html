Your smart(phone) pet!

Your own pet ... in your pocket

Yamayachi is an Android Application made in Android Studio. Whole Project was started as a part of university course. Main idea was to created interactive pet, that will play with you any time you want. Pet is fully interactive, It responds to your touch, based on your phone position it will always keep its eyes on you, and bark at you if you make it happy. Background music will help you relax in your free time but you can turn it off in settings it you want to.

Features

Responsive design

Based on Constraint layout in Android Studio.
Works with any screen resolution

Customized settings

Choose you favourite background for your pet.

Background Music and Sounds

In app background music.
Also Barking sounds.

Interactive

Pet feed your pet!

Programmatically generated graphics

Based on the reading of your gyroscope.
